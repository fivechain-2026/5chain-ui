Why the core is billing

A 3PL handles five clients fine. At thirty, billing breaks.

Storage by volume, charges per pick and pack, returns to inspect, rate cards that change mid-month - reconciled by hand in Excel, the night before invoices go out. Mistakes cost the relationship. Missed charges cost the margin. No WMS on the market solves this cleanly - so we made it the heart of ours.

5 clients
Manageable

A few tabs in a sheet. One person keeps it in their head. It works - barely.

15 clients
Spreadsheets multiply

Rate cards drift. Storage math gets fuzzy. Month-end starts eating whole days.

30 clients
Revenue leaks

Charges go uncaptured. Disputes pile up. The 3PL is financing its own clients without knowing it.
