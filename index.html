Why this exists

A 3PL handles five clients comfortably. At thirty, billing becomes a spreadsheet nightmare.

Storage by volume, charges per pick and pack, returns to inspect, rate cards that change mid-month — reconciled by hand in Excel, the night before invoices go out. Mistakes cost the relationship. Missed charges cost the margin.

How it works

Events in. Invoices out. Nothing typed twice.

5Chain sits on top of the operations you already run. Feed it events from your WMS, a CSV, or our floor app — it does the rest.
